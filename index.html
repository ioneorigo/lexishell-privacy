Privacy Policy for LexiShell

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Introduction

Thank you for using LexiShell.

LexiShell is an offline dictionary application that allows users to import, manage, and use dictionary files on their own devices. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles information and protects user privacy.

By using LexiShell, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection

LexiShell does not collect, store, transmit, sell, rent, or share personal information.

The application does not require user registration and does not request personal data such as names, emails, phone numbers, addresses, or precise location.

Local Data Storage

Dictionary files, search history, bookmarks, settings, and other data are stored locally on the user's device. This data is never transmitted to the developer or third parties.

Internet Access and External Websites

LexiShell may optionally access external websites such as Wikipedia, online dictionaries, translation services, and user-configured search providers.

When a user performs an external search:

  • The search term may be sent directly to the selected third-party website.
  • This happens only due to the user's explicit action.
  • LexiShell does not collect or store this data.
  • LexiShell does not receive information from those websites.

All interactions with third-party websites are governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

User-Configured External Services

Users can create and manage custom dictionary links and search providers. These services are fully controlled by the user.

The developer does not verify, monitor, or take responsibility for third-party services.

Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

  • No analytics
  • No ads
  • No tracking
  • No telemetry
  • No user profiling

Data Sharing

LexiShell does not collect personal data, therefore no data is shared with third parties.

Children's Privacy

LexiShell does not knowingly collect any personal data from users of any age.

Security

All data remains on the user's device unless explicitly exported. Users are responsible for protecting their own device and data.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be published on this page.
